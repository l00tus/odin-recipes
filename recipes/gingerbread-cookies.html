Gingerbread cookies

Description

These gingerbread cookies have the perfect blend of caramelly brown sugar, sweet honey, and a warm balance of spices that instantly brings holiday cheer.

They're literally the perfect Christmas cookie recipe to make and then bring to wherever the party is!

Ingredients for the cookies

  • one third cup light brown sugar/dark brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1 egg
  • half cup honey
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • one quarter cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp. ground ginger
  • half tsp. ground all-spice
  • 2 tbsp. baking powder
  • half tsp. salt

Ingredients for the icing

  • half cup powdered sugar
  • about 2 tsps. milk
  • one eighth tsp. vanilla extract
  • one quarter tsp. food coloring (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix the wet ingredients
  2. Make the gingerbread dough
  3. Roll the gingerbread cookie dough
  4. Cut out the gingerbread cookies
  5. Make the icing
  6. Decorate the cookies
  7. Serve
